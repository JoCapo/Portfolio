About me

My name is Josephine Caporini and I am currently a graphic design student at UCF. I chose to come to Orlando because I felt like there would be more creative opportunities nearby after I graduate. I am in the emerging media major and my hobbies include drawing and photo manipulation.

My Work

In the future I hope to be a graphic designer, and make posters, logos, and other designs. I enjoy working with color and typography, in digital mediums, and I hope I get to do more of it in the future. I take inspiration from music, old comics, and bright colors, which I try to incorporate into my work.
